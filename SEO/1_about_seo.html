SEO - SEO, which stands for Search Engine Optimization, is a digital marketing strategy and practice aimed at improving a website's visibility and ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs). The primary goal of SEO is to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to a website by optimizing various aspects of the site to align with search engine algorithms. SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is important because it: 1. Increases organic website traffic from users actively searching for information or products. 2. Offers cost-effective marketing compared to paid advertising. 3. Builds credibility and trust with users. 4. Enhances user experience and engagement on your site. 5. Provides a competitive advantage in your industry. 6. Targets specific audiences for better conversion rates. 7. Delivers long-lasting results and adapts to changing user behavior. 8. Enables global reach for businesses. 9. Offers measurable results through analytics tools. 10. Ultimately, helps your website rank higher in search engine results, driving more potential customers to your site.
