A favicon, short for "favorite icon," is a small icon or image associated with a website or web page. Favicons are typically displayed in a web browser's address bar, next to the page's title in browser tabs, and in bookmarks or favorites lists. They serve as a visual representation of a website and help users easily identify and recognize the site when multiple tabs are open or when browsing bookmarks.
